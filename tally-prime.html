Quick to set up, ease to use

Setting up TallyPrime accounting software is incredibly easy. In just a minute, you'll have the application ready and can start creating invoices right away. Its intuitive design makes it easy to set up and learn. With TallyPrime, you'll enjoy a consistent and user-friendly experience that helps you discover its features effortlessly.

Detailed, actionable, and tailored reports

With TallyPrime's diverse business reports, you can gain useful insights into various aspects of your business. These reports help you keep track of cash, inventory, and relationships with ease, making it simple to navigate and make better decisions for your business's success.

Get More Done with TallyPrime!

Simplified Your Accounting with TallyPrime

TallyPrime simplifies your accounting tasks with features like automated reconciliation, seamless compliance reporting, and effortlessly managing GST, TDS, TCS, and GSTR-1 by uploading your documents—TallyPrime takes care of the rest.

E-Way Bill and E-Invoice Generation

TallyPrime streamlines GST compliance with automated e-way bill and e-invoice generation (Bkg link). The software integrates directly with the GST portal, enabling you to process single or multiple invoices without manual intervention.

Powerful Reporting for Better Insights

With an intuitive reporting engine, TallyPrime offers features like Change View, Filters, Save View.

  • Filters in Reports: Easily apply filters to narrow down the exact results you need.
  • Change View: Explore alternative views of reports for deeper insights.
  • Save View: Personalize your reports by creating and saving multiple perspectives.

TallyPrime Server

TallyPrime Server adds powerful server-based capabilities to the TallyPrime Gold license, making it ideal for medium and large enterprises. Its advanced Architecture ensures greater concurrency, secure data access, and real-time monitoring capabilities. TallyPrime Server helps growing businesses enhance efficiency, streamline operations, and maintain robust data security.
