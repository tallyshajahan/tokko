About Us

About Tokko

We are a leading provider of cloud solutions, backup, and security solutions, serving businesses with reliable and scalable technologies. Our services include enterprise cloud solutions like VPS (private servers), GPU, Tally on Cloud, and specialized support for platforms like Busy and Genius on Cloud, ensuring seamless access and performance for your operations.

As a trusted Tally solution partner, we deliver the latest Tally Prime software with cloud support and WhatsApp integration, helping businesses stay efficient and compliant. Our dedicated backup services safeguard your data through advanced security and disaster recovery measures. From startups to large enterprises, we deliver comprehensive solutions that drive business success.

Our Vision

To be the leading technology partner for businesses worldwide, empowering them with innovative cloud solutions, comprehensive training programs, and reliable IT services that drive growth and efficiency in the digital age.

We envision a future where every business, regardless of size, has access to cutting-edge technology solutions that streamline operations, enhance productivity, and ensure data security.
